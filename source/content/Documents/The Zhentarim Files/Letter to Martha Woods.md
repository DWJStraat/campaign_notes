Dear [[Marth Woods]]

You've expressed concern about the way the [[Green Serpents|Green Serpent]] has steadily been increasing their activity and carving out their own place in the black market. Under the leadership of an individual such as [[Blightbreath]] they might be a threat to the Zhentarium.

And though I see how it would seem like a threat to our economic stability, we are after all losing a part of the market share to them, but it is not such a clear loss of monetary gain. While your concerns are warranted, you simply do not see the full picture. In an audience with [[Scarlet Vandran]], we have been granted with the vision our Lord [[Cyric]] asks of us, years ago. Periods of strife are coming, yet they are not strife for the [[Zhentarim]], if we are prepared for the difficulties ahead. Chaos within Baldur's Gate does mean the demand for poison might not be as high, yet it also means that we can capitalise off of the chaos. Worry not, instead prepare.

Yours in sincerely,
