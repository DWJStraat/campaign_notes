I figured I'd let you know about the so-called '[[Fangs of Talona]]', or at least that's what Garro calls himself and his merry band. They were fodder for the ritual, circles carved into their back. After that came down, he was so gracious to ask of me a degree of service through forceful means, a blood kiss of sorts. This was also around the time they started to worship [[Talona]], supposedly she helped them survive. This also drew in others, like [[Altesu]] and [[Deanna]], and relations with the [[Green Serpents]]. And through other circumstances, Mallqui eventually joined as well, though she's mainly meant to keep an eye out.  
While I know mostly about the Fangs, and their members, Garro has spoken about more organisations that work directly or indirectly to further Talona's goals, mainly 2 of them. But the nature of these organisations, who runs them and why they work for the goals of Talona is beyond me, merely that they exist. So I will not discuss these any further. I suppose the most important matter is that you should have the tools to defeat these vampires, something which I cannot assist in, but something which I'd still like to see you do. Perhaps there's an old part of me that still wishes to see you succeed, or my capricious nature gets the better of me. But the rites carved into Davna and Garro's back, once you start speaking the words written upon the [[Black Mass Scroll]], it should affect them to a degree. Though the question would be whether you'd want to even finish the ritual.
