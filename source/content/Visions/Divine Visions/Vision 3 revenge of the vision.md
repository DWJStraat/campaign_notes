The vision starts with the feeling of pressure, heavy weight. It is hard to pin down. Almost like my 

body has decided to increase in weight by a ridiculous degree until I couldn't move. Like a statue.



This vision is less clear than the last vision. This is a quick flash of a vision- with an adrenalin to it. 
These flashes are of a red, active liquid, almost like it is stirring itself. The liquid is in bottles. Flasks 
and bottles. Some even in small dishes. The dishes are the most still parts of the liquid. The flashes 
are over in 5 seconds before darkness overtakes me temporarily. 



Next, I feel a disturbance ~~in the force~~ of dust being disturbed by heavy boots in a chamber that 
was sealed for a long time. 




As it fades out, I feel another layer of feeling, especially regarding the liquid. An intrusive sensation 
of wrongness within the deepest parts of my draconic features. My scales feel sensations I have 
never experienced before. Wrong sensations.
