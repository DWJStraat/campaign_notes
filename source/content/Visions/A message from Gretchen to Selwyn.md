Blood, blood and gore. The mists find themselves descending upon the hall. The Skann brother stands alone upon the roofs, yet many are already dead, choked on vampire's mist. The [[Davna Skann|Skann sister]] finds a more personal angle to work, hostages and invitations are attained for the ultimate goal of something sweet. Yet the assassin prepares for the proper strike. Moving unseen yet with the most important payload, the matter of where he strikes as important as when. Yet he does not keep to the Skann's vampiric schedule. He finds the population centres most of all.
