The Hold of Tourm is a hold specialised in containing relics of draconic nature, such as undead wyrms. Years ago, they organized an exchange with the Avowed for the safeguarding of the Living Dragon Plague. This exchange went almost perfectly, though one vial was lost in a raid. All other vials were accounted for and safeguarded in the highest security vault, and put under the protection of the deities Tiamat, Null, and Bahamut. These have been under the Hold's control ever since, though expedition are sent out regularly to acquire samples on resurfacing strains of the plague. 

There is an imprisoned demilich that came with the exchange, which knows a lot about the living dragon plague. It is not quite sane.
