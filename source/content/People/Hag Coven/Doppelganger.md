Gentle Reposed by Dorn on the 4th of Flamerule

Speak with Dead at 4th of Flamerule

Q: What are your ties to the hag known as Auntie Ethel, and how did you come to be at the tavern
A: I do not have any ties to Auntie Ethel.

Q: How did you come to be at the tavern
A: I was ordered by my employer to guard the entrance to the basement.

Q: Where is Deanna
A: Deanna's place of residence is within the feywild

Q: Who it serves
A: I am employed by an individual known as Old Dreamy Deanna
